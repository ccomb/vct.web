My tasks and agenda

Version 0.6, 14 June 2010, (Considered specifications not yet implemented in this prototype are presented in italic).
  • Introduction:
    • An overview of what I am expected to do as user.
  • Tasks to do:
    • Worklist:
      • An overview of tasks to be done as soon as possible.
      • One or several worklists.  For example:
        • In a department of a lab the current list of samples waiting to be processed.
        • For a nurse the current list of patient needing a specific procedure.
        • For a senior doctor the current list of reports or discharges letters to be reviewed and validated.
        • ....
      • Content of a worklist:
        • A list of activities to be performed.  For example:
          • A list of lab test waiting for results.
          • When the task is achieved and validated the task may disappear from the worklist.
    • Reminders:
      • The same principle but appearing only after a given delay.
        • For example:
          • If not yet done, review the situation of a patient after one week of tests.
  • Agenda:
    • Also a planning of tasks, but  to performed at specific times.
    • Integration of an existing agenda system available in open source.
  • Availability:
    • Definition of vailability for tasks and for time frames.
