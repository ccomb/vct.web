Patient identification

  • How to search for a patient:
    There are many ways to search for patient records:
    • (New Search) by means of decriptive identification, including last name, name, bithday, sex
    • Search by means of a record number, inside an identification space as e.g. the record record number system of a particular medical center.
    • Retrieval of several groups of patients as proposed in the drop down list.   Patient are proposed here as far as the user is already allowed to open these records.
  • Results of the serach:
    • All these methods provide a table of zero, one or several candidates with a summary of key information.  Be careful to check the right patient.
  • Opening a patient record:
    • The next step is to click on the appropriate candidate in order to enter inside the detailed record.
      You may also click on (next) or (previous) in order to move in the current group of candidates. For example the next appointment of the day.
    • Access to a patient record depend on the profile of the current user, as explained in more details in the chapter about security.  
      Normally the user must already be a member of the care team of the patient.  Some users are allowed to modify the Care Team of the patient.
    • If not yet done, the opening of a new record will automatically close the previous record.
